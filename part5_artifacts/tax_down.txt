**Legal Framework**: A breach occurs when one party fails to fulfill its contractual obligations without a lawful excuse. Breaches can be minor or material, with material breaches allowing the non-breaching party to terminate the contract and seek damages.

**Remedies**:
- **Compensatory Damages**: Recovery for direct losses due to the breach, such as lost profits.
- **Consequential Damages**: Additional damages that were foreseeable at the time of the contract.
- **Specific Performance**: A court order requiring the breaching party to fulfill their contractual obligations.

- **Civil Penalties**: The IRS may impose penalties for underreporting income or failing to file returns.

- **Vicarious Liability**: Companies may be held liable for employees' actions if within the scope of employment.
